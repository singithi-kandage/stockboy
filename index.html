StockBoy

An app for local food economies.

About

Developers: Brett Hall and Singithi Kandage

StockBoy is a mobile app designed to help connect those interested and motivated in supporting locally-grown food and grocery products with the producers and growers in their community. We recognize the impact that consumer participation in the local food system, including encouraging organic farming practices, and creating greater prosperity for local producers, making local food more accessible to interested consumers, and supporting greater community food security.

StockBoy will allow users to use the app as either a customer or a vendor, or both. Vendors may be producers of local food. Vendors will be given the opportunity to register the location of their farm and/or an affiliated pick-up location, along with a short bio about what they do, and some of what they grow.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions!
